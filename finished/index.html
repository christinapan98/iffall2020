about

LVMH Moet Hennessy Louis Vuitton, commonly known as LVMH, is a French multinational corporation and conglomerate specializing in luxury goods, headquartered in Paris, France. The company was formed in 1987 through the merger of fashion house Louis Vuitton (founded in 1854) with Moet Hennessy, which was established after the 1971 merger between the champagne producer Moet and Chandon and the cognac producer Hennessy.
